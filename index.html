Driven by open data

Augrented shows you information based on the public data sets released by the San Francisco Department of Building Inspection, the Rent Board, Assessor's Office, and other agencies. New York City results include information from the Department of Homes and Community Renewal (DHCR), Housing & Preservation Department (HPD), rodent inspections, Environmental Control Board applications, permits and penalties, tax and water liens, and many other sources.

While we're grateful to these agencies for making the data available, please note that Augrented is not made or endorsed by any city or state agency.
